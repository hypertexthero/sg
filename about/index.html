Simon Griffee

Like many human beings I communicate in one of our oldest languages, drawing, with one of our great tools, photography. Photographing helps me feel and notice the world and time I live in and communicate something of this to other people.

I was born in South America, have traveled and lived in a few places and don’t feel particularly attached to any particular land or label. I’m Currently surviving in New York City.

I’m available for hire for reportage, events, lifestyle, travel, fashion and portrait photography work. I understand the needs of every client are different. When you contact me tell me the kind of coverage you’re looking for and the kind of rights you need for the photographs. I’m happy to discuss your project to best meet your budget while producing work which makes you and your own clients very happy. I’m based in New York City and am happy to travel, which I do lightly with small, professional cameras.

Although most of my projects are long-term, personal ones, I have experience with reportage, fashion and portraiture work, and am available for work. I’m a graphic and web designer and am good with Lightroom, Photoshop, Mac OS, etc. If you feel we may work well together please get in touch.

Though I do all my own editing I like working with editors and art directors and am available for assignments for both short and long-term projects. I’ve also worked for over a decade as a graphic & web designer and understand the requirement of photographs to meet particular layout and style needs for best print and web publishing.
