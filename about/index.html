Simon Griffee

Like many human beings I like to communicate in one of our oldest languages, drawing, with one of our great tools, photography. Photographing helps me feel and notice the world and time I live in and convey something of this to others.

Born and raised in Brazil, my childhood was spent on the move there. I learned English in Tunisia and to see in Italy. I currently live in New York City where I work as a graphic designer in Milton Glaser’s studio and at Columbia University.
