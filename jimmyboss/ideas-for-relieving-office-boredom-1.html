Ideas for Relieving Office Boredom #1

Create a BARRICADE across a corridor of the organization where you work. Use desks, chairs, cabinets, computer monitors, whiteboards and anything else from your office, or storage room you have access to, that you can pile up.

Then, hide behind your barricade and PEEK OUT when people appear in the corridor.

When somebody gets curious and approaches, JUMP UP AND DOWN behind the barricade and scream at the top of your lungs:

OOOOOOOIIIIIIIIIIIiiiiiiiii!!! (MP3 sound file, 60kb)

Hold your position as long as you can.

If you like, send a picture of your barricade along with the time you managed to stay in position to Jimmy and it will be included here.

By the way, it’s Talk Like a Pirate Day!
