Sethro Godino needs to become artiste already and SHUT THE FUCK UP about marketing bullshit!

There’s this bone-head called SETHRO GODINO1 who has nothing better to do than write on his FUCKING blog all the FUCKING time.

But GODINO writes stuff that doesn’t make any FUCKING sense too like:

“Loving what you do is almost as important as doing what you love, especially if you need to make a living at it.”

WTF?
