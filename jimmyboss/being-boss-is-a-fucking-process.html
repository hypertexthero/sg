Being Boss is a Fucking Process

Hey.

In light of recent comments by Andreino Vinciguerra Jimmy thought he’d clarificate some things.

Being Boss doesn’t necessarily mean having your own Organization and telling people what to do. No. Being Boss means believing in yourself and doing what you want to fucking do. But it doesn’t necessarily happen overnight. There’s a fucking process:

  1. Believing you can Be Boss
  2. Wanting to Be Boss
  3. Working at Being Boss

One and two can happen PRONTO, but they usually take a while, especially if you’ve been brainwashed by too much shit, which unfortunately is pretty common. Gotta DEPROGRAM yourself a lot of the time before you can Be Boss.

Number three can take some time.

On his path to Being Boss, Jimmy hustled in the tough streets of São Paulo, Tūnis, London, Bournemouth, Rome and Oakland to name a few. He got lucky a few times. He slacked out and knocked back a few too many on others. But it didn’t matter, ‘cause he knew that he was working towards Being Boss, and he didn’t forget that he was working towards Being Boss.

Jimmy planned it out. In sketches, lists and notes in notebooks in trains and buses. In his mind in the cold, dark, empty streets. In the warm, noisy bars. Jimmy was fucking confident. He didn’t believe when other people told him no. He did it anyway. His way. Like a Boss.

Too many get lost forever in a fucking office down a long corridor of a fucking grey anonymous building where life gets squeezed out of living people. They become ZOMBIE.

Why be ZOMBIE when you can Be Boss?!

Out!!!
