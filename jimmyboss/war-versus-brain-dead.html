War Versus Brain Dead!!!

Hey. Don’t get FUCKING BRAIN DEAD. If you work in a bureaucratic organization, you have to ESPECIALLY WATCH OUT. When you look at the abyss, the abyss stares back. Who said that again? Jimmy doesn’t remember, but it’s true. Spend time with people you like. Spend time with people who inspire you. Spend time with, and for, YOURSELF. Don’t waste time with anything else. And if you need to, maybe because of money, then make sure you got the goal SET in your head, with DEADLINE. And then don’t let DEADLINE SLIP AWAY FOREVER. ‘Cause forever can be tomorrow if you get stuck in a fucking BED IN INTESIVO CARE the moment after the previous moment!!!
