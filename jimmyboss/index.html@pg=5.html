Overcooked Pasta

Jimmy was tired.

He had a lot to do, and when he was doing it he often forgot the pot of pasta cooking on the stove. This meant that when Jimmy was readu to eat, he was faced with monstrous mush instead of al dente delight. This added to Jimmy’s troubles instead of helping soothe them, and Jimmy became angry.

Jimmy decided to make a partnership with some ‘hero’ sort and build a SOLUTION to the fucking problem of overcooked pasta — a fucking problem which affects millions of people, especially busy people and people outside of Italico peninsula.

The solution is now ready, and Jimmy is happy to present you Pasta Clock : Al dente, not al mush.
