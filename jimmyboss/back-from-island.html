Back From Island

When Jimmy was meandering about on an island last week the reason why he likes life in and around the center of the Italic Peninsula came to him:

People here don’t take things too seriously. Laws and rules, time and appointments, governments and religions. All that bullshit is laughed away over dinner. People enjoy life. Like Jimmy.

Wine, pasta, fresh fish, friends, waves, sunsets, doing nothing but what you want to do. These are important things, and here they are the rule.

Jimmy’s back and ready to attack with Jimmy and Franco material among other stuff starring BKG, Donnie, DJKeg et al. Wild bonanza. No rules except the one above. Stand by for photographico madness and be nice.
